About

Planner House is an innovative technology company that provides a floor plan and home design software that is easy to use and extremely powerful.

Hi, my name is Trond and I’m the CEO of PlannerHouse. I’m excited to see so many of you joining our community of floor plan and home design enthusiasts. The PlannerHouse App is an easy-to-use floor plan and home design tool that lets you create floor plans, furnish and decorate them and visualize in impressive 3D.

PlannerHouse was founded in 2007 as a spin-off from a company that creates state-of-the-art 3D visualizations for the sub-sea oil industry. The same technology that is used to visualize large oil rigs below water was applied to visualize the interior of homes and properties. The vision, which we stay true to to this day, was that everyone should be able to visualize their home, online.

Today, we are proud to say that we have more than five million users and customers, coming from more than 170 countries. To this day, more than 7 million projects have been created using the RoomSketcher App. Thousands of small, mid-sized and large businesses, agents, resellers, designers, and contractors have chosen to partner with RoomSketcher. This is well beyond our imagination when we started out.
